## Can I use this resource on a monetized or whitelisted FiveM server?

Yes.

You may use this resource on servers that:
- Require paid applications or whitelisting
- Accept donations, subscriptions, or Patreon support
- Monetize access to the server or community as a whole

Provided that:
- The resource itself is not sold or licensed
- The resource is not advertised as a paid perk, rank benefit, or product
- Access to the resource and its features is not restricted by payment

The resource must be available equally to all players on the server.

## Can I accept donations, Patreon support, or subscriptions?

Yes, provided that donations or subscriptions:
- Do not unlock the resource
- Do not unlock features of the resource
- Do not gate access to the resource
- Are not marketed as payment for the resource

Donations or subscriptions may not be used as:
- A requirement to use the resource
- A condition to access its features
- A benefit or perk tied specifically to the resource

- Sell the resource directly

- Offer the resource as a bonus, perk, or incentive for purchases, ranks,

This applies regardless of whether the package is listed as free or paid.
