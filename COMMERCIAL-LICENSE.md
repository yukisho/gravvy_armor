## What Requires a Commercial License?

A commercial license is required if you intend to use this resource as part of:

- A paid product or service
- A commercial script, framework, or bundle
- A marketplace or storefront offering (including Tebex)
- A value-adding component of something being sold or licensed

This does **not** apply to:
- Monetized FiveM servers
- Paid applications or whitelisting
- Donations or Patreon support unrelated to access to the resource
